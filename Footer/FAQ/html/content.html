General

To login, click on the "Settings" tab in the header, or use the login option from the home screen. Enter your email and password to access your account.

To change your payment method, go to Settings and navigate to the Grown-Ups section. Click on "Payment" and select "Edit" to update your payment information.

To change your username, first click on the 'Settings' tab located inside the header. Next, navigate to the 'Profile' section and click on the 'Edit' button that corresponds with 'Name'.

Privacy

We use industry-standard encryption to protect your personal data. Your information is never shared with third parties without your consent.

Go to Settings, then the Grown-Ups section. Click on "Account" and select "Manage Account" to access account deletion options.

Troubleshooting

Ensure you have an internet connection and try refreshing the page. If the issue persists, try clearing your browser cache or contact support.

Grant camera permissions when prompted. Ensure adequate lighting and hold the QR code steady in front of the camera. If it still fails, try a different browser.

Accessibility

Yes, we strive to ensure our app works well with major screen readers. If you encounter issues, please contact us so we can improve.

You can adjust text size through your device or browser settings. We are working on built-in accessibility controls for future updates.
