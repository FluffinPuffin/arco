Title: How to Hold a Violin

Video Transcript: The violin is most commonly held with the left hand. The violin should rest solidly on the player's left shoulder, settled where the chin rest is right under the player's jaw. The violin should be held level with the floor, not pointing down, and not pointing up. In the beginning, we have three tapes that are played with the fingers of our left hand: Our pointer finger is considered the first finger, it presses on the first tape! Our middle finger is our second finger, it presses on the second tape. Our ring finger is our third finger, it presses on the third tape! Eventually you may have four tapes, but for these lessons, we'll focus on the first three.
