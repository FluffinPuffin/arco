Recap

  • Hold the violin with your left hand
  • Rest the violin on your left shoulder
  • Place the chin rest under your jaw
  • Keep the violin level with the floor

Learn the proper way to hold a violin, including hand placement and posture for comfortable playing.

The violin is most commonly held with the left hand. The violin should rest solidly on the player's left shoulder, settled where the chin rest is right under the player's jaw. The violin should be held level with the floor, not pointing down, and not pointing up.
