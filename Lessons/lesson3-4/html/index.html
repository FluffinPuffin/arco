Title: What is a scale

Overview: A scale is a set of notes beginning at one note and ending at the next note of the same name (open D to high D for example). Major scales follow a specific pattern of whole steps (W) and half steps (H). This pattern is: W W H W W W H. A whole step on a violin is when there is a big space between the fingers, like from B to C#. A half step is when the fingers are close together, like from C# to D.
