Recap

  • The treble clef marks the staff
  • Notes go on lines or in spaces
  • Lines: E, G, B, D, F (bottom to top)
  • "Every Good Boy Does Fine"

Learn about the musical staff, treble clef, and how notes are placed on lines.

This is the treble clef. It marks the beginning of the musical staff. Notes can be placed on the lines, or inside the spaces. The lines from bottom to top are: E, G, B, D, F. Remember: "Every Good Boy Does Fine" - each word starts with a line note! The spaces from bottom to top are: F, A, C, E. Remember: "Spaces spell FACE!" - an easy way to memorize!
