Title: The Musical Staff and Notes

Overview: (Show the treble clef) This is the treble clef. (An image of the staff is on the screen. On each line starting from the bottom: E, G, B, D, F). This is the musical staff. Notes can be placed on the lines, or inside the spaces. These are the lines of the staff. A good way to memorize the lines is to say the phrase “Every Good Boy Does Fine”. The first letter of each word matches each line. (An image of the musical staff is on the screen. In each space starting from the bottom: F, A, C, E). These are the spaces of the staff. A good way to memorize the spaces is to think of the word FACE. Remember, “spaces spell FACE!”.
