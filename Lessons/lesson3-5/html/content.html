Learn the D major scale - one of the most important scales for violin players.

The D major scale begins on a D note and follows the major scale pattern until the next D note in the musical alphabet. The D major notes are as follows: D, E, F#, G, A, B, C#, D. Notice how the scale has two sharps: F# and C#. This is one of the most common scales you'll play on the violin!
