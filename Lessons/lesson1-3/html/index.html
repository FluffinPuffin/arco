Title: How to Hold a Bow

Video Transcript: The bow is most commonly held in the right hand. All five fingers work to hold the bow securely. The thumb and middle finger meet to create a circle. This circle is placed in the gap found in the frog. The pointer finger rests on the grip, lightly hooked for stability. The ring finger wraps over and covers the eye. The pinky is rounded, and sits right on top of the bow stick.
