Title: Fingerboard Map

Overview: The notes on each string follow the musical alphabet. The musical alphabet starts at A and ends at G, then it loops back to A! Every time we go through the musical alphabet, the notes become higher pitched. Every time we press down on a string, the note changes into the next note in the musical alphabet. For example, if you press one finger on the A string, it will change into the note B. The G String: The notes are as follows: A is the first finger, B is the second finger, C is the third finger. The D string: The notes are as follows: E is the first finger, F# is the second finger, G is the third finger. The A string: The notes are as follows: B is the first finger, C# is the second finger, D is the third finger. The E string: The notes are as follows: F# is the first finger, G# is the second finger, A is the third finger.
